Entry Fee & Prize Pot
Each player pays a £10 entry fee. The total pot is awarded to the winner. In the event of a tie, the pot is split equally between tied players.
2
Picks & Replacements
Each player must select exactly 12 celebrities. If a selected celebrity dies before 1 January 2026, that pick may be replaced. All replacements must comply with all eligibility rules.
3
Eligibility Restrictions
Picks must be real, identifiable individuals (no fictional characters or aliases). Players may not select themselves, immediate family members, or anyone they could reasonably influence the death of.
4
Health Restriction
Players may not select anyone with a known long-term or terminal illness at the time the pick is made.
5
Prohibited Conduct
Players cannot kill their own picks.
6
Scoring
For each valid death during the game window: Score = 100 − age at death. Anyone aged 100 or over scores 0 points.
7
Death Verification
Deaths must be confirmed by reputable news sources, with BBC News used as a primary reference where available.
8
Game Window
The game runs from 00:00, 1 January 2026 to 23:59, 24 December 2026. Deaths occurring outside this window do not count.
